<p>这是在运行旧版本 Ubuntu 或 Debian 的计算机上安装 Nala 的首选且最简单的方法，因此您所要做的就是按照下面列出的步骤按列出的顺序进行操作。</p>

<div data-rehype-pretty-code-fragment=""><pre style="background-color: #282c34" tabindex="0" data-language="bash" data-theme="default"><code class="code-highlight"><span class="code-line line-number" line="1"><span data-line=""><span style="color: #56B6C2">echo</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #98C379">"deb [arch=amd64,arm64,armhf] http://deb.volian.org/volian/ scar main"</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> | </span><span style="color: #61AFEF">sudo</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #98C379">tee</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #98C379">/etc/apt/sources.list.d/volian-archive-scar-unstable.list</span></span></span></code></pre></div>

<div data-rehype-pretty-code-fragment=""><pre style="background-color: #282c34" tabindex="0" data-language="bash" data-theme="default"><code class="code-highlight"><span class="code-line line-number" line="1"><span data-line=""><span style="color: #61AFEF">wget</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #D19A66">-qO</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #98C379">-</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #98C379">https://deb.volian.org/volian/scar.key</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> | </span><span style="color: #61AFEF">sudo</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #98C379">tee</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> </span><span style="color: #98C379">/etc/apt/trusted.gpg.d/volian-archive-scar-unstable.gpg</span><span style="color: #ABB2BF"> > </span><span style="color: #98C379">/dev/null</span></span></span></code></pre></div>
